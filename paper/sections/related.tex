\section{Related Work}
\label{sec:related}

\paragraph{Chain-of-thought analysis and LLM-output evaluation.}
A growing body of work analyses chain-of-thought (CoT) reasoning traces
for safety-relevant patterns: faithfulness
benchmarks~\citep{shen2026faithcot, song2025prmbench}, hint-utilisation
taxonomies~\citep{chen2025reasoningmodels}, and monitorability
arguments~\citep{korbak2025cotmonitor}, building on evidence that CoT is
often unfaithful~\citep{turpin2023biased, lanham2023faithfulness} and
that LLMs exhibit sycophancy~\citep{sharma2023sycophancy} and
reward-tampering behaviour~\citep{denison2024subterfuge,
perez2022discovering}; \citet{lee2025stepwiseeval} survey the area. All
evaluate models against a category set fixed in advance.

\paragraph{Analysing populations of reasoning traces.}
A separate line studies whole collections of traces but still imposes a
fixed or per-prompt schema. Thought
Anchors~\citep{bogdan2025thoughtanchors} classifies sentences into eight
predefined step types; \citet{li2026schoenfeld} import a four-part
taxonomy from Schoenfeld's problem-solving theory; and
\citet{li2026performative} argue many reasoning steps are formatted for
an evaluator instead of driving computation. Closest to us,
\citet{tyagi2026globalcot} cluster traces into strategy graphs, but have
a language model write a per-prompt keyword dictionary that a substring
matcher then applies. \textsc{TaxonomyAgent} instead induces the category
set from an unlabelled corpus and one goal instruction, with no fixed
schema and no per-prompt dictionary.

\paragraph{Multi-agent annotation and codebook construction.}
Multi-agent systems build codebooks collaboratively:
Co-DETECT~\citep{codetect2025}, last year's EMNLP demonstration, refines
a seed codebook with a human in the loop;
Thematic-LM~\citep{qiao2025thematiclm} runs coder, aggregator, and
reviewer agents; TAMA~\citep{xu2025tama} adds expert oversight; and
\citet{liu2025aiannotorch} use LLM verifiers as a quality layer over LLM
annotators. \textsc{TaxonomyAgent} sits at an orthogonal operating point:
it discovers a candidate taxonomy from scratch with a single goal
instruction, no seed sketch and no human in the loop, then hands off the
trace, working taxonomy, and per-item classifications for downstream
review. The orchestrator-and-judge separation reduces the multi-agent
pattern to a single ReAct loop.

\paragraph{LLM topic discovery and taxonomy induction.}
On topic discovery, TopicGPT~\citep{pham2024topicgpt} prompts an LLM to
generate topic labels, TnT-LLM~\citep{wan2024tntllm} stages a
summarise-then-distil pipeline that anticipates our
generator-and-labeller split, and
BERTopic~\citep{grootendorst2022bertopic} remains the strongest non-LLM
baseline. Concurrent 2025 systems induce taxonomies through LLM
clustering, grounded-theory coding, or hierarchy
construction~\citep{brady2025iterativetax, pi2025logos,
zhu2025contextawaretax, yang2025llmitl, huang2025clustasclass}, and
adjacent threads target goal-driven clustering, concept induction, and
taxonomy adaptation~\citep{wang2023goalex, lam2024lloom, taxoadapt2025,
llmguided2025}; \citet{tan2024llmannot} survey the landscape. None expose
a goal-instruction mechanism that lets the analyst steer discovery toward
a non-topical axis of variation, the gap \textsc{TaxonomyAgent} addresses.
