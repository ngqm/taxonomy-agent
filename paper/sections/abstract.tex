We present \textsc{taxonomy\_agent}, an open-source system-demonstration tool that
discovers and refines a labelled taxonomy over an unlabelled text corpus through
a two-tier orchestrator--judge agent. A capable orchestrator LLM proposes
edits in a small typed revise vocabulary (add, rename, edit, merge, split,
drop) which are validated before mutation; a cheap parallel judge LLM scores
items and surfaces don't-fit signals that drive convergence. The system
ships with native OpenRouter cost accounting, partial-progress saving, and a
