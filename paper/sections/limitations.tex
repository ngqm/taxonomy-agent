\section*{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

20~Newsgroups predates current LLM training cutoffs and may be partially memorised; on DarkBench the six manipulation categories carry surface-lexical signal, so a lexical baseline (BERTopic) already reaches NMI $0.746$ and the benchmark measures recovery of the expert category set more than it isolates manipulation type from vocabulary; the don't-fit convergence signal is calibrated on the test corpora and not on held-out data; one 20~Newsgroups seed entered a monotonic-add loop on first attempt and was resolved by re-sampling plus the auto-finalize fallback (\S\ref{sec:eval-results-20ng}).
