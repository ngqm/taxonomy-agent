\section*{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

20~Newsgroups predates current LLM training cutoffs and may be partially
memorised. On DarkBench the manipulation categories carry
surface-lexical signal, so a lexical baseline (BERTopic) already reaches
NMI $0.746$ and the benchmark measures recovery of the expert category
set more than isolation of intent from vocabulary. The don't-fit signal
is calibrated on the test corpora, not held-out data; and one
20~Newsgroups seed needed re-sampling plus the auto-finalize fallback
(\S\ref{sec:eval-results-20ng}).
