\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We presented \textsc{taxonomy\_agent}, an open-source orchestrator--judge
agent for iterative taxonomy discovery with a typed revise DSL, a
self-reported and externally-validated convergence signal, and
operational features (cost tracking, partial-progress saving, a
Streamlit UI) that make it usable as a real tool rather than a
research artefact.
